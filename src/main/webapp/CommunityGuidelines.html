Introduction

Welcome to ClubSphere, the platform where students connect, collaborate, and grow through club activities. These Community Guidelines are designed to ensure that ClubSphere remains a safe, respectful, and inclusive environment for all users.

By using ClubSphere, you agree to abide by these guidelines and help us maintain a positive community atmosphere. We believe that everyone deserves to participate in club activities free from harassment, discrimination, and other harmful behaviors.

Our Commitment

At ClubSphere, we are committed to:

  • Inclusivity: Creating an environment where all students feel welcome and valued, regardless of background, identity, or beliefs
  • Safety: Protecting our community from harmful content, harassment, and abusive behavior
  • Respect: Fostering mutual respect and constructive communication among all members
  • Transparency: Being clear about our policies and how we enforce them
  • Growth: Supporting personal and professional development through meaningful club experiences

Community Standards

To maintain a positive community environment, all users must follow these standards:

Be Respectful

  • Treat others with kindness and respect, even when you disagree
  • Value diverse perspectives and experiences
  • Use inclusive language and avoid stereotypes

Be Constructive

  • Share feedback in a helpful and supportive manner
  • Focus on ideas and content, not personal attacks
  • Contribute positively to discussions and activities

Be Authentic

  • Use your real identity and accurate information
  • Be honest in your interactions and communications
  • Take responsibility for your actions and words

Be Responsible

  • Follow university policies and applicable laws
  • Respect intellectual property and copyright
  • Help maintain a safe environment by reporting violations

Prohibited Content and Behavior

The following content and behaviors are strictly prohibited on ClubSphere:

Harassment and Bullying: Any form of harassment, bullying, threats, or personal attacks targeting individuals or groups

Hate Speech: Content that promotes discrimination, prejudice, or violence against individuals or groups based on protected characteristics

Inappropriate Content: Sexually explicit content, graphic violence, or any material that would be considered inappropriate in an educational setting

Spam and Scams: Unwanted commercial content, phishing attempts, or deceptive practices

Privacy Violations: Sharing others' personal information without consent or engaging in doxxing

Illegal Activities: Content that promotes or facilitates illegal activities or violates university policies

Impersonation: Creating fake accounts or impersonating others, including university staff or organizations

Reporting Violations

We encourage all users to help us maintain a safe community by reporting violations of these guidelines. Here's how to report:

  • In-App Reporting: Use the report button on any post, comment, or user profile
  • Email Reports: Send detailed reports to support@clubsphere.edu
  • Phone Support: Call our support team at +1 (555) 123-4567 during business hours

What to Include in Your Report: When reporting, please provide as much detail as possible, including screenshots, usernames, dates, and descriptions of the violation. This helps us investigate and take appropriate action more effectively.

All reports are taken seriously and investigated promptly. We will take appropriate action based on the severity and nature of the violation, which may include content removal, warnings, temporary suspensions, or permanent bans.

Enforcement

We enforce these guidelines fairly and consistently. Consequences for violations may include:

  • Warnings: Formal warnings for minor first-time violations
  • Temporary Suspension: Temporary account suspension for repeated or more serious violations
  • Permanent Ban: Permanent account termination for severe or repeated violations
  • University Referral: Referral to university authorities for violations that may violate student conduct policies

The severity of consequences depends on factors such as the nature of the violation, the user's history, and the impact on the community. We reserve the right to take any action we deem necessary to protect our community.
